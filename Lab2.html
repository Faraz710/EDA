gold_ts<-ts(gold$Price,start = min(gold$Month),end = max(gold$Month),frequency = 1)
par(mar=c(2,2,2,2))
plot(gold_ts)

There is upward trend. Time series with trends, or with seasonality, are not stationary

acf(gold_ts)

The ACF declines gradually from 1 to 0 over a prolonged period of time, then the series is not stationary

pacf(gold_ts)

adf.test(gold_ts)
## 
##  Augmented Dickey-Fuller Test
## 
## data:  gold_ts
## Dickey-Fuller = -2.3526, Lag order = 3, p-value = 0.4359

Arima Model for GDP dataset

gdp=read.csv("gdp.csv")

par(mar=c(2,2,2,2))
plot(gdp_ts)

acf(gdp_ts)

The ACF falls immediately from 1 to 0, then equals about 0 thereafter, the series is stationary
